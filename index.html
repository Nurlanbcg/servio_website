Features

Everything You Need to Run Your Restaurant

From order-taking to inventory management, Servio covers every aspect of your restaurant operations.

Real-Time Updates

WebSocket-powered live updates ensure all staff see order changes instantly — no page refreshes needed.

Smart Order Management

Take orders from tables, send them to the kitchen or bar, and track every order through its lifecycle.

Inventory Tracking

Monitor stock levels in real time. Automatic ingredient deduction when orders are placed and alerts for low stock.

Menu Management

Create and manage menu items with categories, pricing, and ingredient associations — all from an intuitive admin panel.

Role-Based Access

Secure authentication with role-specific views and permissions for Admin, Waiter, Kitchen, Bar, and Cashier.

Mobile-First Design

Fully responsive interface optimized for tablets and phones — perfect for waitstaff taking orders at the table.

Workflow

How Servio Works

A seamless flow from customer order to payment, with every role connected in real-time.

1

Customer Orders

Waiter takes the order at the table using the mobile-friendly interface. Items are selected from the menu with a few taps.

2

Smart Routing

Food items are automatically sent to the Kitchen display, while drinks are routed to the Bar — no manual sorting needed.

3

Preparation

Kitchen and Bar staff see live order queues. They update statuses as items are being prepared and marked as ready.

4

Delivery & Payment

Waiters are notified when orders are ready. Cashier handles the checkout with a complete order summary and total.
